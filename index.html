About Us

At Luban Workshop, we blend traditional craftsmanship with modern culinary innovation. Named after the legendary Chinese master carpenter Lu Ban, our restaurant embodies the same dedication to precision, quality, and artistry.

Our chefs meticulously craft each dish using locally sourced ingredients and time-honored techniques, creating an unforgettable dining experience that celebrates both tradition and innovation.
