Our Story

A restaurant built around craft, learning and welcome.

Luban Workshop Restaurant is more than a place to order a meal. Located at UCC in Cape Coast, it is a dining space where authentic Chinese cuisine meets the energy of a practical hospitality workshop.

Every service gives our team the chance to practise the details that matter: careful preparation, steady timing, thoughtful presentation and attentive guest care. The result is a restaurant experience shaped by both discipline and heart.

Inside Luban Workshop A calm campus dining space for students, staff, families and visitors in Cape Coast.

What Guides Us

What we want every guest to feel.

Craft in the Kitchen

Our menu is rooted in Chinese cooking techniques, balanced flavours and the steady discipline required to serve each dish with consistency.

Learning by Doing

As a workshop restaurant, we support practical hospitality training through real service, real teamwork and real guest expectations.

Hospitality with Care

From reservations to the final plate, our aim is to make guests feel looked after, respected and comfortable at every step.

The Experience

A thoughtful Chinese dining experience in Cape Coast.

Guests come to Luban Workshop for flavour, but the experience is bigger than the plate. We care about the rhythm of service, the comfort of the room and the pride our team brings to each visit.

Freshly prepared dishes Soups, seafood, beef, rice, noodles, dumplings and more prepared with close attention to timing and presentation.
A campus location with community reach Based at UCC, we welcome students, staff, families, visitors and food lovers from across Cape Coast.
Reservations and events Beyond daily dining, we support table reservations, private gatherings, corporate meals and catered occasions.
