Our Story

A restaurant built around craft, learning and welcome.

Luban Workshop Restaurant is more than a place to order a meal. Located at UCC in Cape Coast, it is a dining space where authentic Chinese cuisine meets the energy of a practical hospitality workshop.

Every service gives our team the chance to practise the details that matter: careful preparation, steady timing, thoughtful presentation and attentive guest care. The result is a restaurant experience shaped by both discipline and heart.

Inside Luban Workshop A calm campus dining space for students, staff, families and visitors in Cape Coast.

At The Table

The Experience

A thoughtful Chinese dining experience in Cape Coast.

Guests come to Luban Workshop for flavour, but the experience is bigger than the plate. We care about the rhythm of service, the comfort of the room and the pride our team brings to each visit.
