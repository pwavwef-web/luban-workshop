# Launch Checklist

## Before Posting

- Confirm campaign start date.
- Confirm current menu/order link.
- Confirm reservation/contact link.
- Confirm catering contact process.
- Pick the final hashtag set.
- Check all captions for price or availability changes.
- Export feed graphics at 1080 x 1080 px.
- Export story graphics at 1080 x 1920 px.
- Add alt text for accessibility.

## First Post

- Use `luban-mascot-pose-01-wave.png`.
- Caption: use Caption 1 from `captions.md`.
- CTA: menu link in bio or post comment.
- Story support: add a poll asking what Bao should recommend first.

## Daily Workflow

- Publish feed/story post.
- Reply to comments within the same day.
- Save common questions for FAQ/story follow-up.
- Track link clicks and enquiries.
- Repost user mentions when appropriate.

## End Of Campaign

- Compare menu clicks before and during campaign.
- Count order, reservation, and catering enquiries.
- Identify the top three posts by reach and engagement.
- Reuse the best-performing pose in future ads.
