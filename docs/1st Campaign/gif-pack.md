# Bao GIF Pack

This folder contains animated GIF assets for the **Meet Bao** campaign.

Recommended use:
- Sticker GIFs: comments, WhatsApp status, story overlays, and lightweight reaction posts
- Social-post GIFs: square Instagram/Facebook feed posts
- Story GIFs: vertical Instagram/WhatsApp story graphics
- Reaction GIFs: short caption-friendly responses and campaign replies
