# Creative Brief

## Campaign Look

Warm, polished, food-forward, and friendly. The visuals should feel like a restaurant campaign, not a children's show.

## Visual System

Primary mascot:
Use the transparent PNG pose set from `assets/mascots/poses/`.

Primary colors:
- Deep red: `#b91c1c`
- Red-brown: `#7c2d1f`
- Warm ivory: `#fff6e8`
- Dumpling cream: `#fff1d7`
- Gold accent: `#d9a441`
- Stone text: `#292524`

Layout guidance:
- Keep the mascot large enough to recognize.
- Leave clean negative space for menu, verified-phone order, or reservation text.
- Use warm ivory or stone backgrounds.
- Use red-brown seal-inspired accents sparingly.
- Use one clear CTA per design.

## Feed Post Template

Size:
1080 x 1080 px

Structure:
- Mascot on left or lower-right
- Food or icon accent near the bottom
- Headline in upper open space
- CTA button or QR marker near bottom

Suggested copy:
Headline: Meet Bao
Subcopy: Your guide to warm Chinese meals at Luban Workshop Restaurant.
CTA: View Menu

## Story Template

Size:
1080 x 1920 px

Structure:
- Mascot in lower third
- Big question or CTA in the middle
- Link sticker area near bottom

Suggested story copy:
What should Bao recommend today?
Noodles / Dumplings / Fried Rice

## Flyer Template

Size:
A5 portrait or 1080 x 1350 px

Structure:
- Mascot on lower-left
- Food or steamer image along bottom
- Headline and offer in top half
- QR code and CTA at bottom-right

Suggested flyer copy:
Meet Bao at Luban Workshop Restaurant
Chinese cuisine, warm hospitality, and meals made for sharing.
Scan to view menu.
Online pickup orders require a verified Ghana phone number. Email verification is optional.

## Design Rules

- Do not place text across the mascot face.
- Do not add fake Chinese characters.
- Do not stretch the mascot PNGs.
- Do not put the mascot on very busy photos.
- Keep shadows subtle if added by designers.
- Keep all icons at consistent stroke weight when used together.
