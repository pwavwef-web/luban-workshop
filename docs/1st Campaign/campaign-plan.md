# Campaign Plan: Meet Bao, Your Luban Guide

## Campaign Goal

Launch the Luban Workshop Restaurant mascot as a recognizable social media character and use him to increase menu visits, orders, reservations, and catering enquiries.

## Campaign Idea

Bao appears as a warm, helpful dumpling chef who guides customers through Luban's food experience: what to try, where to order, how to reserve, and why the restaurant is built around both Chinese cooking and hands-on hospitality training.

## Key Message Pillars

1. **Warm Welcome**
   Bao invites people to the restaurant with friendly poses and simple food-first messaging.

2. **Menu Discovery**
   Bao highlights noodles, dumplings, fried rice, steamer baskets, and signature dishes.

3. **Campus Convenience**
   Bao makes pickup, lunch breaks, group meals, and quick menu browsing feel easy.

4. **Workshop Craft**
   Bao connects the food to careful preparation, service practice, and hospitality learning.

5. **Group Moments**
   Bao promotes sharing: friends, families, student groups, events, and catering.

## Tone

Friendly, warm, polished, appetizing, clear, and lightly playful.

Avoid:
- Overly childish copy
- Fake Chinese characters
- Stereotypes or cultural caricatures
- Too much text on visuals
- Generic food stock-photo language

## Campaign Structure

Duration: 14 days

Main phases:
- Days 1-3: mascot introduction
- Days 4-8: menu discovery and ordering prompts
- Days 9-11: reservations, group meals, and catering
- Days 12-14: reminder push and engagement
