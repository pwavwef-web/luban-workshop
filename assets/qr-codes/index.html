Print Recommendations

Size suggestions:

  • Minimum: 2cm × 2cm (0.8" × 0.8") for reliable scanning
  • Recommended: 5cm × 5cm (2" × 2") for easy scanning from distance
  • Large displays: 10cm × 10cm (4" × 4") or larger for window signage

Print settings: Use high contrast (black on white background) and ensure images are not compressed.
