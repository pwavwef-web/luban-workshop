A staff-only resource page with downloadable QR codes for marketing and in-restaurant use.

Restaurant Main Page QR Code

Links to the homepage. Great for: table signage, flyers, window displays, business cards.

Digital Menu QR Code

Links directly to the menu. Perfect for: menu cards, table stands, promotional materials.

How to Use QR Codes

The QR codes page is located at: lubanrestaurant.com/assets/qr-codes/

✓ You can:
  • Download QR codes as PNG images
  • Copy the direct URL to QR code images
  • Print them at various sizes (2cm to 10cm+)
  • Place them on menus, signage, and promotional materials

Print Recommendations

  • Minimum size: 2cm × 2cm (0.8" × 0.8")
  • Recommended: 5cm × 5cm (2" × 2")
  • Large displays: 10cm × 10cm (4" × 4")
  • Always use: High contrast (black on white)
  • Avoid: Compressing or distorting the image

Marketing Ideas

  • Print QR codes on table placemats linking to the menu
  • Add QR code to takeout bags/boxes linking to website
  • Create flyers with QR codes for local distribution
  • Display QR code at the entrance for online ordering
  • Include QR code on receipts to leave reviews or make reservations

Troubleshooting & Common Questions

Website Access Issues

What if the website won't load?

Check: 1) Internet connection is working 2) Browser is up to date 3) Try a different browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox). The website URL is: lubanrestaurant.com

What if pages load slowly?

This could be due to: 1) Slow internet connection 2) Mobile network signal. Try switching to WiFi or moving closer to the router. Refresh the page to try again.

QR Code Issues

QR code won't scan

Make sure: 1) The code is printed at least 2cm × 2cm 2) Code is high contrast (black on white) 3) Not folded, damaged, or distorted 4) Phone camera is clean and in focus.

Customer Questions You Might Receive

Q: Why is the site sometimes slow?

A: A slow connection from WiFi or mobile data can affect loading times. The website itself is optimized for performance. Try refreshing the page or connecting to a faster WiFi network.

Q: Can I pay online through the website?

A: Check with management on current payment options. Share the contact information so they can inquire directly.

Q: Why don't you deliver?

A: Explain our dine-in and takeout options. For special requests, direct them to use the contact form or speak with management.

Q: Can I see the menu in a different language?

A: The menu is currently in English. If translation is needed, ask them to speak with management about special requests.

What to Do If Something Breaks or Changes

  • Document what's not working (screenshot if possible)
  • Note the page URL and what the issue is
  • Report immediately to management or the website administrator
  • In the meantime, provide alternative assistance (verbal info, printed menu, etc.)
