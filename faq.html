About Luban Workshop

Luban Workshop Restaurant serves authentic Chinese cuisine, including soups, beef, seafood, rice & noodles, and more. Our menu draws on traditional Chinese culinary techniques and flavours.
Luban Workshop Restaurant is located on the University of Cape Coast (UCC) campus at Cafe Roof Top, Casford Street. It serves as both a hospitality training ground for UCC students and a premier dining destination for guests in Cape Coast, Ghana.
Yes. Luban Workshop functions as both a hospitality training facility and Cape Coast's premier fine-dining destination. It provides hands-on training for hospitality and culinary students at UCC while delivering an exceptional dining experience to all guests.

Reservations

You can reserve a table directly on our Private Events & Catering page by completing the reservation form. Alternatively, call us on 020 543 8455 during opening hours.
Reservations can be made up to 3 months in advance through our online form.
As a hospitality training facility at UCC, our culinary students carefully plan each day's service to maximize learning and minimize food waste. Because our team begins preparing fresh ingredients for your arrival well in advance, we kindly request at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or modify your reservation. Please contact us at 020 543 8455 or use our online contact form. Your timely updates directly support our educational mission and help us run a sustainable kitchen.

Online Orders

Yes. You need to sign in to place an order online. You can still start signing up with your email address, but placing an order now requires a verified Ghana phone number. Email verification is optional.
Every order at Luban Workshop provides a valuable, hands-on learning opportunity for our students. To ensure technical precision while strictly preventing food waste, our team begins preparing your meal almost immediately. Because of this active preparation, orders can only be cancelled online within the first 5 minutes. If your plans change after this brief window, please call us directly at 020 543 8455. We will gladly accommodate your cancellation request, subject to staff approval, provided our kitchen has not yet started cooking your ingredients.
Once signed in, you can view your order history in the "My Orders" section of the main page.

Food & Allergies

We take allergies very seriously. Please inform our staff of any dietary requirements or allergies when you arrive or when making a reservation. Some dishes can be adapted on request.
Yes! We offer a range of vegetarian dishes on our menu. Please ask a member of staff for recommendations or check our online menu for dishes marked as vegetarian.
